### ✅ T137 — [P0] Close the within-tenant privacy leak on `anonymous_messages` (F05)
Surfaced auditing RLS before public launch: `anonymous_messages` shipped with the standard `apply_standard_rls` policies, so "members read" (`is_member`) let every co-member SELECT every row including `sender_user_id`, and "comitet write" (for all) gave the comitet the same blanket per-row read. Backend-free regression guard.
**Done:** new additive idempotent migration `20260526000001_anonymous_message_privacy.sql` drops `"members read"` + `"comitet write"` on `anonymous_messages` (keeping the self-scoped `owner manage` from `reapply_owner_rls`, which is what lets a resident submit/withdraw/export their own rows).
